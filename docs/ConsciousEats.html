ConsciousEats

Source:
  • common/ConsciousEats.js, line 3
Version:
  • 2021/22
Author:
  • Avyay Jamadagni

ConsciousEats.js is a module to create a game where 2 players develop sustainable eating habits. INSTRUCTIONS: Players choose dishes for each meal and move around the board based on the sustainability rating of the dish they chose. Each dish has tags associated with them, and if they collect 5 tags of the same kind, they earn a badge. If the damages of their selected dishes decrease with every meal, that is, they are eating sustainably, they earn a powerup which is immediately applied. The game ends when one of the players reach the end. The sustainability score is added to the number badges they have, and the winner is then decided.
